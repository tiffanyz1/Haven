ABOUT HAVEN

MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is to educate women about how to handle an assault and provide options for them to find open and crowded places to limit their chances of being assaulted. We even provide advice on how to properly defend yourself if need be as every young women should have a sense of security anywhere.

According to the National Domestic Violence Hotline , “One in 6 women (16.2%) in the United States have experienced stalking victimization at some point during their lifetime in which they felt very fearful or believed that they or someone close to them would be harmed or killed (by any perpetrator)”.



ABOUT US

We are a group of 17 year old girls who like many women in the world, experience street harassment in one form or another. With this website we developed and designed, we hope to relieve women around the world from experiencing harassment or fear.



QUICK GUIDE

Here is a quick guide on how to navigate your way through Haven. When you first arrive at the site, you are taken to the homepage. There you can click on multiple icons to either sign up, login, or see some helpful tips and additional info. This website was designed to be User-oriented and easy to use. We hope that this website can help you stay educated and safe.

Thank you! Yours Truly, The Creators
